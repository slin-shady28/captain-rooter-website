About Captain Rooter

A 24-hour plumbing crew based in West Town, serving all of Chicago.

Plumbing problems don't keep business hours — so neither do we. Captain Rooter Emergency Plumbers runs around the clock from our shop at 1252 W Chicago Ave, answering calls across the city at any hour, any day.

Our approach is simple: show up fast, find the real problem, and explain it plainly before any work begins. Whether it's a sewer line check, gas pipe work, or a toilet that picked the worst possible moment to fail, you'll know what's wrong, what it takes to fix it, and what it costs — before we start.

Our technicians treat your home like their own: professional, concerned, and determined to leave with results.
